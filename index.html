Top 3 activities to do in tetouan

OLD MEDINA

The medina is Tetouan’s historic heart, protected by five kilometers of crenellated rampart walls and seven magnificent doors.

MUSEUM OF MODERN ARTS

The city’s creativity is not restricted to the arts and crafts of the past, however. Tetouan is also home to one of only two modern art museums in Morocco, the other being located in the capital city of Rabat.

NEARBY COAST

The city is a 20-minute drive from the sea, where a host of fishing villages, ports and beach resorts await exploration.
